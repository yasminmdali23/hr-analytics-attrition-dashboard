# HR Analytics & Employee Attrition Dashboard

A human resources data visualization dashboard built in Power BI to evaluate workforce demographics, track employee retention rates, and uncover underlying causes of corporate attrition. This dashboard bridges corporate culture with data analytics to protect talent retention.

## 📊 Key Metrics & Insights
* **Total Workforce Headcount:** 1,470 employees
* **Total Voluntarily Attrited:** 237 employees
* **Average Attrition Rate:** 16.1%
* **Active Corporate Employees:** 1,233
* **Average Staff Age:** 37 years old

## 📈 Features & Visualizations
* **Department-Wise Attrition:** Donut chart separating employee departures across business divisions (R&D experiencing the highest raw loss at 56.12%).
* **Age & Gender Demographics:** Segmented column charts mapping staff counts by age tiers (peaking between 25-34 years old) with comparative gender splits.
* **Education Field Attrition:** Horizontal bar rankings measuring attrition impact across background degrees (Life Sciences leading at 89).
* **Workforce Sentiment Matrix:** A detailed satisfaction rating table (Scores 1 to 4) cross-referenced directly against individual job roles like Sales Executives and Research Scientists.
* **Attrition Rate Progression:** Radial progress ring metrics isolating exactly how gender and age buckets interact across the attrition curve.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
* **Power BI Desktop:** Data transformation, Matrix Visuals, Progress Ring indicators, and HR KPI Dashboard Architecture.

## 📂 How to Use
1. Download the `.pbix` file.

3. Use the top education tier buttons (Associate, Bachelor's, Doctoral, Master's, High School) to instantly filter the organization's retention analytics.
